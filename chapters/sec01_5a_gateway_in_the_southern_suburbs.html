Chapter 5:

A Gateway in the Southern Suburbs

All this time, the parallel development of Gateway Kids’ School of Computing continued apace. Branches offering supplementary classes in computing for children had been established at various centres around the country. One of these was at Vajira Road, Bambalapitiya, not far from Visakha Vidyalaya. The building that housed it was a large colonial bungalow with many rooms to spare. In 1999, a Gateway preschool was established on the premises, offering classes up to Year 2.

In 2008, Noreen Welikala, who had served as principal of Stafford International School for more than twenty years, joined Gateway as a consultant. Seeing great potential in the Bambalapitiya branch, she applied her wealth of experience to its growth and development. By 2012, the school had expanded with the addition of classes for Years 3-6, which were housed in a second building across the street.

This somewhat unsuitable arrangement (the difficulties of which were exacerbated by the massive access and parking problems generated by next-door Visakha Vidyalaya) ended in 2013, when Gateway took over the former American International School in Dehiwala. Under the direction of Ms Welikala, the Bambalapitiya branch was merged with the American International School and Gateway College Dehiwala was born. Meanwhile, the building at Vajira Road was renovated and restored to its former elegance. Now named the Guildhall, it has become the venue of choice for Gateway social and community events.

Technology is an integral part of the students’ learning experience.

Gateway College Dehiwala has many modern facilities that promote holistic development.

A centre of excellence

Ms Welikala, who had chosen to retain her consultant status, retired in 2016. Her close colleague Priyanthi Seneviratne succeeded her as principal of Gateway College Dehiwala and holds the post to the present day.

By 2017, Gateway Dehiwala had over nine hundred pupils and catered for every scholastic grade from infants’ playgroup to IGCSE and A-level, allowing pupils to receive a complete school education at one establishment. The school building is a capacious seven-storey structure with every modern amenity including a state-of-the art auditorium, an indoor basketball court and a swimming pool.

Gateway encourages innovation and creativity.
