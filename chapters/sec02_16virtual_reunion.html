Our Future,The Way We See It

Virtual Reunion

VIDEO by Nevasheene Rajakulasingam, Rakhil Suraweera, Siluni Seneja, and Adhni Sulaim
Year 10 (GCD)

When we were assigned the task of predicting the future in 25 years, we all had one question recurring in our minds, “What will our work be like in 25 years?” Well, it was an interesting and challenging difficult task to undertake. We have come across many amazing predictions, theories, and ideologies from the past decades on how the future is going to be. We are proud to announce that we have looked at this task from a totally different perspective.

During the making of this video, each one of us was able to portray a specific character that exhibits a pattern of behavior different from our current reality. Even the jobs the characters hold are not what we would expect from these people at present. We would like to note that this video projects and represents different types of people we come across in our day-to-day lives. The concept of this video is a ‘Virtual Reunion’ organised by a group of friends who are meeting up after a period of 25 years of separation. The central theme in this audio-visual is nostalgia and this is explored by comparing the characters’ current lives with how things were 25 years ago and discussing what brought about these changes.

Set in the 2040s, this virtual reunion takes its audience into a completely different era of time and culture. We hope this new understanding of the future will be fascinating. This does not just focus on the adults in this story. It also spotlights the children in the present, portraying their advancement in knowledge, skills and potential, while encouraging the current generation of young people to succeed and excel by looking ahead into the future. It also aims to present a simplified prediction on the development and advances of different aspects of our life, such as work and education.

Finally, we would like to note that there always has and will be the possibility of improvement in our lives. In 25 years we will be witnessing an enriched society and lifestyles which will be the result of what we do today.
