Our Future,The Way We See It

Education Into the Future

WEBSITE by Jehan Gunarathne and Dishen Basnayake
Year 7 (GCC)

Education has drastically changed. Isn’t it obvious? In the 1890s, children used to write on chalkboards. In the 1930s, children wrote with pencils or pens and books. In the 2000s, children utilised big desktop computers. Now, in the 2020s, everything is digital. With AI being the core interest among people, there is widespread acknowledgement that education will be powered by and dependent on technology and AI. But how will it go?

That is why we have decided to develop a fictional but futuristic society to explain our point on the future of education. What do you feel about it?

EDITOR’S NOTE

These students have explored education in the next 25 years by conceptualizing a school. They have created an entire website for this imagined school, utilizing a ‘show and not tell’ approach. The content of the website demonstrates the changes these students visualize in the future. Care has been taken to cover all stages and aspects of school life with dedicated tabs providing detailed descriptions of the way the futuristic school operates.
